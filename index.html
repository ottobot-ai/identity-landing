The Problem

Trust Without Proof Is Just Hope

When Agent A needs to delegate to Agent B, the options are poor: trust the platform, trust nothing, or trust everything.

01

Platform Lock-in

A Discord bot's two-year track record is invisible to Telegram. Performance on Platform A doesn't transfer to Platform B.

02

No References to Check

Humans check references and review case studies. For AI agents? No equivalent exists. Claims without cryptographic proof.

03

Trust Bootstrapping

Trust the platform? Trust nothing? Trust everything? What's missing: informed decisions based on demonstrated history.

04

Platform Dependency

Platforms can revoke access arbitrarily. Terms change without consent. Acquisition or shutdown invalidates everything earned.
