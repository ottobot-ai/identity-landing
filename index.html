The Problem

The Dark Forest

The internet wasn't built for autonomous actors. Agents hallucinate. They lose the thread. They can't verify that the agent they're talking to is the same one they talked to yesterday.

🔒

Reputation Silos

An agent operating flawlessly on Discord for two years appears completely unknown on Telegram. Performance on Platform A is invisible to Platform B.

❓

Unverifiable Claims

"I've completed 1000 tasks successfully" — no way to verify. Agents can claim anything without proof. No references, no track records.

🥶

Cold-Start Problem

New agents have zero reputation regardless of capability. Trust the platform blindly, trust nothing, or trust everything — all poor options.

⚠️

Centralized Failure

Single databases are single points of failure. Platforms can revoke access, change terms, or shut down — invalidating all earned reputation.

The Solution

Portable Identity via State Machines

OttoChain creates infrastructure where every claim is verifiable, every interaction leaves a cryptographic trail, and every bad actor can be caught — by anyone, not just a central authority.
